Former resident of Dakar hospitals (1995), we specialized in Urology at Cheikh Anta DIOP University in Dakar. We followed additional training in Andrology and organ transplantation at the University of Lyon I in France. During this period we held the post of associate assistant in Urology where we received practical training in endourology. Back in Dakar we took the competitive examination in medicine and participated in the teaching of urology. We held the position of head of department of surgery of the faculty of medicine odontology and pharmacy of the University Cheikh Anta DIOP from 2011 to 2015 and as such we represented the faculty from 2012 to 2016 in the Tunning Africa project (Partnership Union African and European Union) aiming to harmonize higher education in Africa. Head of the andrology urology department at A le Dantec hospital (2019). We are elected president of the Senegalese urology association of 2020

Research

We are authors and co-authors of a number of publications focusing on urology (endoscopy, oncology, urological emergencies, kidney anatomy, andrology and malformations)
